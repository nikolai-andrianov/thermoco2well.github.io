Presentation at the SPE Reservoir Simulation Conference

  • February 26, 2023February 26, 2023

The SPE Reservoir Simulation conference is one of the major events for everyone, who is into numerical simulation of subsurface flows. So we were very happy to learn that our presentation with the first results from the project has been accepted as an oral contribution!

So if you happen to be in Galveston, Texas on March 29, pass by the session on CO2 Storage and Enhanced Geothermal System Modeling to attend our presentation
